Toronto, Ontario

About

Toronto is located next to Lake Ontario in Canada.

Some facts about Toronto include:

  • Toronto is the 4th most populous city in North America.
  • The current mayor is John Tory.
  • Established in 1793, known as "York".
  • Visit its website at www.toronto.ca!

Statistics

  • About 50% of the population was born outside Canada.
  • Home to around 26% of the entire Canadian population.
  • 17.5% of the population is 14 or under.
  • The gender population is 48% male and 52% female.
